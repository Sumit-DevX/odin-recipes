Zucchini Tots

Description

These veggie-packed carrot and zucchini tots are crispy, crunchy, and deliciously stylish - because kids eat with their eyes too!

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 cup grated zucchini
  • ¼ cup grated carrot
  • ¼ cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • ¼ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ⅛ teaspoon dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spray a mini muffin tin with cooking spray.

  2. Combine zucchini and carrot in a large bowl. Squeeze out excess water using a paper towel.

  3. Mix bread crumbs, feta cheese, basil, egg, salt, pepper, and oregano into zucchini-carrot mixture. Scoop mixture with a mini ice cream scoop or two spoons and press into the prepared muffin cups with the back of a spoon.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown on top, 15 to 18 minutes.
